Metal Gear Solid

Metal Gear Solid is an action-adventure stealth video game produced by Konami Computer Entertainment Japan and released for the PlayStation in 1998.

The protagonist is Solid Snake, a legendary Mercenary, that has retired to Alaska. Colonel Roy Campbell, Solid Snake's former commanding officer, calls Snake back in a top-secret mission on Shadow Moses Island. Snake's mission is to infiltrate the nuclear weapons disposal site and rescue the hostages and eliminate the nuclear threat.

During the mission, Snake receives support via codec radio. Colonel Roy Campbell supports Snake with advice and tactics. Dr. Naomi Hunter gives Snake medical advice. Mei Ling, who invented the soliton radar system used in the mission and is also in charge of mission data, the players can call her to save the game. Nastasha Romanenko, who provides item and weapon tips. Master McDonell Miller, a former drill instructor, and survival coach provides survival technics to Snake in the Alaskan weather. Nastasha Romanenko a nuclear and military analyst who provides item and weapon tips. Game controls and strategies can also be accessed through the Codec.

HALO: COMBAT EVOLVED

Halo: Combat Evolved is a military science fiction first-person shooter video game developed by Bungie and published by Microsoft Game Studios. Halo released as a launch title for Microsoft's Xbox video game console on November 15, 2001. Microsoft released versions of the game for Microsoft Windows and Mac OS X in 2003.

Assuming the role of the elite UNSC Spartan soldier known as the Master Chief, Halo: Combat Evolved quickly drops the player into action as the starship Pillar of Autumn is attacked by humanities sworn enemy, the Covenant.

Final Fantasy 8

Final Fantasy VIII is a role-playing video game developed and published by Square for the PlayStation console. Released in 1999, it is the eighth main installment in the Final Fantasy series.
During a time of war, Galbadia a Global Superpower has declared war on the country of Dollet. As a sorceress manipulates the most powerful men in Galbadia, Squall learns that the sorceress's name is Edea, who has been behind Galbadia's recent hostilities. Under orders from Balamb and Galbadia Gardens, Squall and his comrades attempt to defeat Edea. After defeating Edea, the group learns that Edea was under the control of Ultimecia, a powerful sorceress from the future.During a time of war, Galbadia a Global Superpower has declared war on the country of Dollet. Squall, a member of Balamb Garden's mercenary force SeeD, together with his friends and Rinoa, a member of a resistance group, are shipped off to war, unaware of their fated task to save the world. Squall learns that the military nation of Galbadia has allied with Sorceress Edea who has been behind Galbadia's recent hostilities. Under orders from Balamb and Galbadia Gardens, Squall and his comrades attempt to defeat Edea. After defeating Edea, the group learns that Edea was under the control of Ultimecia, a powerful sorceress from the future.
Final Fantasy VIII uses a turn-based battle system, between the playable characters and CPU-controlled enemies. Final Fantasy VIII has 6 playable characters, Squall Leonhart, Rinoa Heartilly, Quistis Trepe, Zell Dincht, Selphie Tilmitt, and Irvine Kinneas. The summoning-creatures in Final Fantasy VIII are called Guardian Force (GF), assigning a GF to playable characters will allow that character the ability to summon that GF.

GEARS OF WAR

Gears of War is a video game franchise created by Epic Games, and owned and published by Microsoft Studios. The first installment, titled Gears of War, was released on November 7, 2006, for the Xbox 360.

Players follow the protagonist Marcus Fenix, a soldier in the Coalition of Ordered Governments tasked to lead a last-ditch effort to destroy the Locust Horde and save humanity. Gears of War is set 14 years after the Locust emerged and attacked Sera's human population. Marcus seeks personal redemption as he leads his fire team against an onslaught of merciless warrior fiends.

In this third-person tactical action/horror game, gamers immerse themselves in an experience so intensely emotional and gut-wrenching that playing will be like controlling a blockbuster action movie.
